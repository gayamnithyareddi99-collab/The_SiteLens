Core Intelligence Modules

Six Modules. One Complete AI Renovation Intelligence Platform.

TheSiteLens combines Readiness Inference, Stage Truth Verification, Defect Detection, Sequence Alignment, Attendance Intelligence, and GDPR-Compliant Documentation into one unified mobile-first SaaS platform no hardware required, real-time, regulation-ready.

Module 01

Readiness Inference Engine

AI determines true operational readiness of every site stage predicting if the next trade can safely begin based on observed site conditions, not unreliable declared statuses.

The Readiness Inference Engine uses semantic segmentation (Mask R-CNN) and object detection to verify if prerequisite works such as first-fix plumbing, electrical chasing, or substrate priming are actually complete. Instead of trusting a subcontractor's verbal update, the AI analyzes smartphone photos to confirm readiness, preventing the £250–£500 wasted cost of every failed trade visit in London renovation projects.
Module 02

Stage Truth Verification & Golden Thread

Automatically generates immutable, time-stamped "Stage Truth" certificates for hidden construction stages waterproofing, insulation, MEP satisfying the Building Safety Act 2022.

For every verified stage, TheSiteLens generates an auditable "Stage Truth" certificate. These tamper-proof records satisfy the UK Building Safety Act's Golden Thread requirements, providing insurers and Building Control officers with legally defensible visual evidence of work-in-place before it is covered by finishes. All certificates are stored in encrypted UK-based cloud infrastructure for full data sovereignty.
Module 03

Defect Detection & Rework Risk Alerts

Identifies structural defects, moisture risks, and improper material usage in hidden areas before they are buried under finishes eliminating latent defect liability.

TheSiteLens Room-State Benchmarking analyzes images for specific Stage Truth indicators and defects: improper waterproofing in wet rooms, insufficient floor preparation for luxury tiling, or substandard insulation behind plasterboard. By benchmarking the current room state against regulatory requirements, the system ensures quality at every transition point before the 2025 Future Homes Standard compliance deadline triggers exponentially higher remediation costs.

Module 01 · Readiness Inference

AI Readiness Inference Engine

Proprietary computer vision models trained to determine the "true operational readiness" of a site using semantic segmentation (Mask R-CNN) identifying 50+ critical pre-finish site elements across MEP, substrate, and structural boarding.

Instead of relying on a subcontractor's declared status, the AI analyzes visual data to verify if prerequisite works such as first-fix plumbing, electrical chasing, or substrate priming are actually complete. This prevents "failed visits" and ensures subsequent trades only arrive when the site is physically ready for them, saving London contractors an average of £250–£500 per avoided wasted visit.
Module 02 · Stage Truth

Stage Truth Verification & Golden Thread

Automatically compiles verified image data into time-stamped, tamper-proof PDF Stage Truth certificates for regulatory compliance satisfying the Building Safety Act 2022 and providing insurers with objective proof.

The Stage Truth Generation Engine provides an immutable, time-stamped record of work-in-place, satisfying requirements of the Building Safety Act and giving Building Control officers the necessary evidence of compliance. This digital audit trail covers waterproofing, insulation, MEP first-fix, and structural milestones reducing long-term liability for the builder and accelerating milestone payment approvals.
Module 03 · Sequence Alignment

Visual-to-Sequence Mismatch Detection

Continuously compares real-time site captures against the planned project schedule (JCT/NEC/MS Project), instantly flagging out-of-sequence work before it is buried under expensive finishes.

The Visual-to-Sequence Alignment Module maps visual site evidence against standard JCT and NEC project schedules to detect deviations or out-of-order works. If the AI detects a stage being bypassed such as tiling before waterproofing is verified it instantly flags the mismatch, intercepting cascading delays and preventing the burial of defects that would otherwise require costly destruction of finished work to remediate.
Module 04 · Defect Detection

Room-State Benchmarking & Defect Detection

Analyzes site images for structural defects, moisture risks, and improper material usage in hidden areas benchmarking against high-quality standards and UK regulatory requirements before transitions.

TheSiteLens leverages Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) to synthesize diverse construction defect and stage-completion samples, ensuring superior accuracy in "messy" real-world site conditions. Issues like improper waterproofing in wet rooms, insufficient floor preparation for luxury tiling, or substandard insulation behind plasterboard are identified before the 2025 Future Homes Standard compliance costs escalate exponentially through hidden remediation.
Module 05 · Workforce Intelligence

Attendance Decision Intelligence

Provides real-time decision support for project managers regarding workforce deployment recommending whether trades should attend or reschedule based on AI-verified site readiness to prevent wasted travel.

By analyzing the progress of all active renovation zones, the AI recommends whether a trade should attend the site or if their visit should be rescheduled to avoid downtime. This module optimizes the deployment of a limited skilled workforce particularly valuable as the UK construction industry faces a critical skills shortage and aging workforce maximizing daily productivity and eliminating the communication loop failures that cause most failed handovers.
Module 06 · Privacy & Compliance

GDPR-Compliant Site Documentation

All site data handled with a privacy-first approach automatically redacting faces and PII from photos while maintaining 100% construction data fidelity in encrypted, auditable UK-based cloud storage.

The Edge-Inference Mobile Interface processes visual captures locally on standard smartphones for instant feedback without requiring high-speed internet critical for basements and rural properties. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit, with an immutable ledger approach to project records. The system design is mapped against the Golden Thread requirements of the Building Safety Act and CDM 2015 regulations, ensuring every verification certificate is legally defensible for Building Control officers and insurance claims.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TheSiteLens Renovation AI and what it means for your building firm's operational efficiency, compliance, and margin protection.

What is TheSiteLens Renovation AI and why does my building firm need it?

+
TheSiteLens Renovation AI is an AI-powered visual intelligence platform that solves the systemic efficiency and transparency crisis in UK residential renovation. The platform converts ordinary smartphone site photos into structured "Readiness Inference" and "Stage Truth" intelligence predicting trade-handover failures before they happen, automatically generating Building Safety Act compliance certificates, and providing objective proof of quality at every hidden construction stage. Unlike passive progress photo apps, TheSiteLens actively prevents the £250–£500 cost of every failed trade visit and the 10–20% rework costs that erode SME profitability.

How is this different from generic project management apps like Trello or Procore?

+
Generic tools like Trello or Procore serve as digital filing cabinets they record what happened but cannot understand or verify what is physically on-site. TheSiteLens is a renovation-specific AI that "sees" and "understands" sites. It can visually verify if plasterboard is ready for a mist coat, if waterproofing is continuous before tiling, or if first-fix MEP is genuinely complete tasks that currently require expensive physical site visits or trust in verbal updates. The platform is architecturally incapable of being replicated by generic project management tools, which lack the computer vision and construction domain intelligence to interpret visual site evidence against planned sequences.

What is "Stage Truth" and why does it matter for my Building Safety Act compliance?

+
Stage Truth is TheSiteLens's term for the AI-verified, time-stamped photographic evidence of construction work at critical "hidden" stages waterproofing behind tiles, insulation continuity behind plasterboard, MEP first-fix routing before plastering. The UK Building Safety Act 2022 mandates a "Golden Thread" of digital information throughout a building's lifecycle, and Stage Truth certificates are specifically designed to satisfy this requirement. For SME builders, these certificates provide legal protection against latent defect liability, help secure insurance coverage, accelerate milestone payment approvals, and demonstrate compliance to Building Control officers without requiring expensive physical inspections at every stage.

Does TheSiteLens require any specialised hardware or cameras?

+
No. TheSiteLens is specifically designed to run on the standard smartphones already in every worker's pocket. There are no 360-degree cameras, no head-mounted sensors, and no specialised scanning equipment required. This eliminates the £5,000–£10,000 hardware barrier that prevents SME adoption of professional ConTech tools like OpenSpace or Buildots. Optional Smart Capture Enhancement Kits (wide-angle lens attachments, stabilised gimbals, and rugged mounting hardware) are available at £450 for clients who want to optimise capture quality in challenging environments like dusty sites or low-light basements but the core platform works with any modern smartphone camera.

How accurate is the AI, and what happens if it makes an error?

+
TheSiteLens has achieved a 91% accuracy rate in identifying Trade Readiness milestones across 12 active renovation zones during pilot testing, successfully predicting 14 potential trade-handover failures in the testing period alone. The system uses GAN-generated synthetic datasets to handle real-world site conditions including dust, clutter, poor lighting, and partially obscured installations. Critical compliance certificates particularly Golden Thread Stage Truth documents require a final site manager sign-off on the AI's verification (human-in-the-loop), ensuring no certificate is issued without human accountability. TheSiteLens is clearly defined in its Terms & Conditions as a decision-support tool rather than a substitute for a qualified surveyor or Building Control inspection.

Is TheSiteLens compliant with UK GDPR and data protection regulations?

+
Yes. TheSiteLens is built with a "Privacy-First" architecture specifically designed for occupied residential renovation environments. The system automatically redacts PII (worker faces, resident personal data) at the point of capture on the mobile device before any image is uploaded. All construction data is stored in encrypted UK-based cloud clusters (AWS London / GCP London), ensuring 100% UK data residency and GDPR compliance. TheSiteLens Ltd is registered with the Information Commissioner's Office as a Data Controller. The platform is also aligned with the EU AI Act 2024 requirements for human-in-the-loop oversight and algorithmic transparency, ensuring all AI-generated readiness scores are explainable and auditable.
